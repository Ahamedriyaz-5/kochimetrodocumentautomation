About Our Journey

Elevating Kochi's infrastructure through cutting-edge digital document management.

Our Story

Kochi Metro Rail Limited (KMRL) is at the heart of Kerala's urban transformation. As we manage thousands of critical records daily—from engineering blueprints to safety protocols—we recognized the need for a seamless, digital source of truth.

The Digital Document Management Hub was born from this vision. We moved away from traditional paper-heavy processes to create a smart, AI-integrated ecosystem that empowers our departments with instant access and total accountability.

Our Mission

To provide a digital-first, secure platform that streamlines document workflows, enhances intradepartmental collaboration, and ensures 100% data integrity for Kochi Metro.

Our Vision

To lead as a globally recognized digital organization, achieving 0% paper dependency through sustainable innovation and operational excellence.
