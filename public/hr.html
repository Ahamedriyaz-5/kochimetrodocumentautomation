Our HR Commitment

Kochi Metro Rail Ltd (KMRL) follows structured HR policies emphasizing contract-based employment, merit-based recruitment, and strict compliance with ethical standards.

Recruitment & Selection

KMRL conducts direct recruitment via official channels only. Applications are accepted exclusively through our online portal.

  • Public Notification: All openings advertised in national newspapers and official website.
  • Fair Selection: Shortlisting followed by written/online tests and structured interviews.
  • Communication: Only shortlisted candidates are notified via registered email.
⚠️ Anti-Fraud Warning: KMRL does not outsource recruitment. Beware of fake job advertisements on social media.

Employment & Tenure

Positions are offered on a fixed-term contract basis, typically for three years, with extensions possible based on performance.

High-level roles such as Director positions are subject to specific age and tenure-related superannuation limits.

General Guidelines & Ethics

  • NOC: PSU/Govt employees must provide a No Objection Certificate.
  • Ethics: Any attempt to influence the selection leads to permanent disqualification.
  • Calculation: Age and experience are calculated strictly as per notification dates.

Compensation & Benefits

Scale (AM) ₹50,000 – ₹1,60,000
Merit Bonus Up to 15% enhancement
Incentives Performance-linked increments
Allowances As per Contract Policy

Compliance Checklist

Mandatory documentation includes Birth Proof, Educational Certificates, Community Certificates (if applicable), and Experience records.
