Comprehensive Safety Commitment

KMRL ensures a secure environment through passenger safety monitoring, worker health measures, and 24/7 operational vigilance.

Operational & Passenger Safety

KMRL conducts regular audits for hazardous conditions, such as unsecured billboards and structural weaknesses, to prevent third-rail obstructions during monsoon periods. We collaborate with IITs and CSIR-SERC for viaduct structural monitoring.

Construction Phase (OHS) Protocols

During Phase II construction, KMRL mandates Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) Management Plans for all contractors. This includes:

  • Monsoon Preparedness: Stabilizing temporary structures and clearing drainage.
  • Community Safety: Managing dust, noise, and vibration in dense urban zones.
  • Traffic Wardens: Deployed to manage diversions and protect pedestrians.

Grievance Redress (GRM)

Passengers and staff can report safety concerns via WhatsApp, Email, or our toll-free hotline.
