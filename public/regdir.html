Operational Profile

KMRL is a 50:50 joint venture between the Government of India and the Government of Kerala. It operates an integrated multi-modal transport network including:

  • Kochi Metro Rail: 28.8 km operational network.
  • Kochi Water Metro: India's first integrated water transport system.
  • Metro Connect: Feeder bus services to airports and hubs.
